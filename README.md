# SKEVA

> Markets Solvent by Construction.

Skeva is a next-generation on-chain derivatives protocol where every position is fully collateralized by its maximum possible loss before execution. By escrowing worst-case risk upfront, Skeva eliminates hidden liabilities, improves capital transparency, and creates a safer trading environment for every participant.

Built on Solana, Skeva delivers transparent, capital-efficient, and verifiable derivatives trading without relying on centralized intermediaries.

---

# 🚀 What is Skeva?

Traditional derivatives markets rely on margin systems that may become undercollateralized during extreme market conditions.

Liquidation delays, cascading insolvencies, and counterparty risk remain major challenges.

Skeva introduces a **Solvency-by-Construction** model.

Instead of relying solely on liquidation engines, every trade escrows its maximum possible loss before execution, ensuring protocol solvency under predefined conditions.

```text
Open Position
      ↓
Calculate Maximum Loss
      ↓
Escrow Required Collateral
      ↓
Execute Trade
      ↓
On-Chain Settlement
```

No hidden leverage.

No unsecured liabilities.

Transparent by design.
